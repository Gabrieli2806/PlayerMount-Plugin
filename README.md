Adds a new interaction between players, allowing you to mount others for fast transportation, gliding together on the same elytra, or just for fun. With built-in sneak dismounting and mounting permissions, it seamlessly enhances both casual and PvP gameplay.

![Showcase](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/VCpoSGc7/images/3cd5ad817d5872ee07cab4c4b3967a5abc1710d8.png)

---

**Features**

*Player-on-Player Mounting*
* Interactive Riding - Right-click on other players to ride them (perfect for shared travel or elytra flights).
* Permission System - Mounting is completely consensual and only allowed if the target player has explicitly enabled it.

*Commands & Visuals*
* `/playermount <true|false>` - Toggle whether other players can ride you.
* `/playermountbossbar <true|false>` - Toggle the visibility of an optional dynamic boss bar that shows your mount's health while you are riding them.

*Dynamic Behavior & Safety*
* Sneak Dismounting - Players being mounted can easily eject their rider by looking up and sneaking quickly 3 times.
* Auto-Removal - Riders are automatically dismounted if the player they are riding disconnects or safely ejects them.
* PvP Protection - Includes built-in protection for riders, preventing them from taking damage from the player they are currently riding.
